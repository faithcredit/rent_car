== Description ==

= VikUpdater =

Keep all of your VikWP commercial products up to date using our own custom VikWP updater plugin!

Visit [VikWP.com](https://vikwp.com/) for more details.

= Keep your products up to date =

Using VikUpdater, you can always download the latest version of your purchased VikWP products. VikUpdater can update:

* VikWP Themes
* Payment Gateways for VikWP plugins
* Any commercial VikWP plugin with no internal update system

= Update with a single click =

After you have registered your VikWP product in the VikUpdater plugin, as long as your product is installed and active on your website, then all it takes to check and update your product is 2 clicks!
You will be able to check for a newer version of the product with the click of a button, and if a new version is available, you will be able to download it right away.
The installation of the new version is automatic after download, and you won't lose any data you have created!
